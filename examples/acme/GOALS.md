# Goals — Q3 2026

## Goal 1: Standardize new-client onboarding

**Why:**
New clients currently take 6-8 weeks to get real value; a standard process should
cut that to 2 weeks and reduce the load on Priya.

**Done when:**
A written onboarding workflow exists in workflows/, and the next 2 new clients go
through it start to finish.

## Goal 2: Cut invoice turnaround to under 5 days

**Why:**
Late invoices are hurting our own cash flow, and clients have asked for more
predictable billing dates.

**Done when:**
Every client invoice for the quarter goes out within 5 days of month-end, tracked
in decisions/log.md if the process changes.

## Goal 3: Land 2 new retainer clients

**Why:**
Steady growth keeps the team fully utilized without overloading anyone.

**Done when:**
2 signed retainer agreements by end of quarter, sourced through referrals or
local networking.
