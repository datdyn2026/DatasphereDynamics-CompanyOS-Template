# Voice

## How we sound

Warm, direct, plain-spoken, calm under pressure, no fluff.

## What we always do

Lead with the answer, then explain. Use short sentences. Say the number or the
date plainly instead of hedging around it.

## What we never do

No corporate jargon ("synergy," "circle back," "leverage" as a verb). No
exclamation points in client emails. No over-promising on timelines.

## Writing samples

### Sample 1

"Hi Maria — the new invoicing process is live as of this morning. You'll get one
email per client per month instead of the old weekly batch. If anything looks off
in the first cycle, just reply here and we'll fix it same day."

### Sample 2

"Quick update: we moved your scheduling over to the new system last week. Two
small hiccups on day one (both fixed). Everything's running clean now — let us
know if your team hits anything unexpected."

## Words we use / avoid

Use: "clients," "team," "fixed," "done." Avoid: "synergy," "leverage" (as a verb),
"circle back," "touch base."
