# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Who we serve best

Service businesses with 10-50 employees — think dental practices, small law
firms, boutique agencies — where the owner is still doing too much admin
themselves.

## Their biggest problem

Billing, scheduling, and reporting are held together with spreadsheets and one
overworked person's memory. Something breaks whenever that person is out sick.

## Why they choose us

We show up with a plan in the first meeting instead of a sales pitch, and we fix
the process using tools they already have — no forced software switch.

## Red flags (not a fit)

Businesses under 5 people (too little to fix yet), or businesses looking for a
software vendor rather than an operations partner.

## Where we find them

Mostly referrals from current clients, plus a handful of local small-business
networking groups.
