# Company Overview

## What we do

Acme Consulting helps small service businesses (10-50 people) fix messy back-office
operations — billing, scheduling, and reporting — so owners spend less time on admin
and more time on clients.

## Business model

We charge a flat monthly retainer ($3,000-$6,000/month depending on scope) for
ongoing ops support, plus one-time project fees for setup work like migrating a
client to new software. No hourly billing — clients told us they hate watching a
clock.

## Stage

Founded 2021. Four people, profitable, steady client base of 14 retainer clients.
Not actively chasing rapid growth — focused on serving current clients well and
adding 1-2 new clients per quarter.

## What makes us different

We embed with a client's actual tools instead of pushing our own software. Most
competitors sell a platform; we fix the operations underneath whatever platform
the client already has.

## Current focus

Rolling out a standard onboarding process so new clients get value in their first
two weeks instead of their first two months. See GOALS.md for the current quarter.
